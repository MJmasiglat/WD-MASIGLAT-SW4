Different Types of Burgers

Classic Cheeseburger

A timeless favorite, the classic cheeseburger combines a juicy beef patty with melted cheddar cheese and fresh vegetables, all sandwiched in a toasted sesame seed bun.

Bacon Avocado Burger

This burger offers a delightful blend of savory and creamy flavors with crispy bacon and creamy avocado complementing a well-seasoned beef patty, topped with spicy mayo for an extra kick.

Veggie Burger

A delicious and healthy option, the veggie burger features a flavorful black bean or chickpea patty, fresh vegetables, and a dollop of hummus or vegan mayo, all served on a nutritious whole wheat bun.
